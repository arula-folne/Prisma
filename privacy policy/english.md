# Prisma Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Prisma, operated by folne ("Operator"), collects and uses information.

## 1. Information We Collect

Prisma may collect and store:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Channel IDs
* Minecraft Java Edition UUIDs

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

Prisma does not collect or store:

* Discord message contents
* Discord direct message contents
* Voice data
* Email addresses
* Passwords
* Payment information

## 3. Purpose of Collection

Collected information is used for:

* Linking Discord and Minecraft accounts
* Authenticating users for Minecraft server access
* Service operation and maintenance
* Preventing abuse and impersonation

## 4. Data Sharing

Collected information is not shared with third parties except when required by law.

## 5. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary to provide the Service.
